About Us

BookingNextTrip is an online travel platform which came into existence based on requirements of tourists and travelers. Our aim is to cater tourists and travelers with comfortable and relaxing stay experience across all destinations in offbeat area North Bengal(Dooars, Darjeeling,Lava, Lolegaon, Rishyap etc), South Bengal (Bankura, Purulia), Sikkim and Bhutan at an affordable price. We provide our customer, a wide range of options to choose, such as best in class Home stays, Cottages, Tree House, Villas, Service Apartments, etc. We also provide sightseeing and transfer facility along with the stay, thus a customer can customize a complete tour package with the assistance of our dedicated team consisting of best travel experts.
